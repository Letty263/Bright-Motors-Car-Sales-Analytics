# 🚗 BRIGHT MOTORS CAR SALES ANALYTICS

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes Bright Motors car sales data to uncover meaningful insights into vehicle sales performance, customer purchasing behaviour, and revenue trends. The objective is to support data-driven decision-making that can improve sales strategy, customer targeting, and overall business growth.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Understand overall car sales performance over time
* Identify top-performing vehicle brands and models
* Analyse customer purchasing behaviour and demographics
* Determine revenue trends and high-performing segments
* Support strategic decision-making for sales and marketing teams

---

## 📊 Key Questions Answered

* Which car brands and models generate the highest sales?
* What are the monthly/quarterly sales trends?
* Who are the key customer segments purchasing vehicles?
* Which factors influence higher sales revenue?
* Are there seasonal patterns in car purchases?

## 🔄 Workflow

1. **Data Collection** – Gathered Bright Motors car sales dataset
2. **Data Cleaning** – Removed duplicates, handled missing values, standardized formats
3. **Data Transformation** – Structured data for analysis using SQL
4. **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** – Identified patterns and trends
5. **Dashboard Development** – Built visual reports for insights
6. **Insights Generation** – Converted findings into business recommendations
